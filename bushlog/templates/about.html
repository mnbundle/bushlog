What's New?

 

Bushlog has finally been launched and although it's still in a beta phase, development has been stepped up and we will be releasing new features fortnightly.

 

Conservation

We believe that the data collected can be used for conservation efforts, and therefor share logged sighting with registered and accredited researchers and conservation organisations. So you are in effect helping with the study and preservation of our precious wildlife.

Bushlog is non-profit and though the contributions of our advertisers we will be able to fund conservation efforts. We believe in a hands on approach to funding such causes, and therefore prefer to sponsor assets, man-hours and the likes rather than making direct cash donations.
