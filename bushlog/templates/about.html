About BushLog

BushLog has been a few years in the making, a lot of that time spent planning, and is essential an interactive application which allows registered users to share their wildlife sightings with other like-minded people.

Stay In Touch

 

Conservation

We believe that the data collected can also be used for the greater-good of conservation, and therefor share logged sighting with registered and accredited researchers and conservation organisations. So you are in effect helping with the study and preservation of our precious wildlife.

BushLog is non-profit and though the contributions of our advertisers we will be able to fund conservation efforts. We believe in a hands on approach to funding such causes, and therefore prefer to sponsor assets, man-hours and the likes rather than making direct cash donations.
